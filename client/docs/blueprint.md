# **App Name**: FinTrack ERP

## Core Features:

- Multi-Company Management: Allows managing multiple companies within a single system with data isolation.
- Role-Based Access Control: Controls user access based on assigned roles (Admin, Manager, Recovery Officer, Accountant) with permission-controlled access to pages.
- Customer & Guarantor Management: Enables adding, editing, blacklisting, and activating customers and guarantors, maintaining detailed profiles, and tracking relationships.
- Product/Plan & Installment Management: Supports creating and managing products/plans with installment schedules, down payments, due dates, and penalties.
- Invoice Lifecycle Management: Manages the complete invoice lifecycle from draft to active, completed, or defaulted status, automatically generating ledger entries.
- Recovery Tracking: Tracks payments and recoveries, allowing partial payments, late fees, rescheduling, and receipt printing.
- Intelligent Insights & Recommendations: Leverage historical ERP data to identify which products sell more frequently for similar customer profiles. Suggest them when a salesperson creates an invoice. The suggestions will appear using an assistant-style "tool".
- Ledger & Accounting: Maintains a double-entry ledger system with running balances, filtering by date, and export functionality.
- Dashboards & Analytics: Provides KPI cards (Total customers, Active installments, Due today, Overdue, Total recovery this month) and charts (Monthly collections, Overdue trend, Sales vs Recovery) for insights.
- Reporting: Generates downloadable reports with filters for recovery, outstanding, cash flow, and sales summary.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Teal (#008080) to evoke a finance and enterprise feel.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5), providing a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Green (#32CD32) to highlight key actions and elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif for a modern, neutral look, ensuring readability and a professional feel.
- Use a set of consistent, professional icons from a library like Font Awesome or Material Icons, aligned with financial and business themes.
- Desktop-first responsive design with a left sidebar navigation, top header, and dashboard-centric layout, similar to modern ERP systems.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience, such as loading spinners, hover effects, and modal animations.
